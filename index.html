About Me

I began my professional career as a civil litigation attorney in Kansas City. After four years of practice I elected to pursue a Bachelor's of Science in Engineering emphasizing in Electrical and Computer Engineering with the eventual goal of eligibility to practice patent law. Pursuant to this goal, and the potential to practice software engineering patent law, I enrolled in The University of Kansas Coding Bootcamp Program to obtain a basic programming foundation to further build upon in the future.

Throughout my engineering studies, I have continued to practice law in my prior capacity. However, I am currently interested in employment opportunities to expand my programming skillset on my journey to transition my career to patent law.
